Instructions:
  • I'm pretty sure this works, but let's call it beta for now. Let me know if you have any issues.
  • Save your work frequently. When you press the 'Save All' button, all fields are saved, then you are returned to your position in the page.
  • In general, whitespace (spaces, tabs, newlines) is ignored. Exceptions are noted.
  • When you see \n, that tells Android to insert a newline. In most cases, you should have \n in the same place in your translation.
  • Similarly, HTML code such as <b> or <i> should probably be used in your translation exactly as they are in English.
  • Don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions. (Contact info listed in the Android Market.)
